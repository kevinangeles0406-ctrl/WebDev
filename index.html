About Spider-Man

Peter Parker - The Amazing Spider-Man

Spider-Man is a superhero created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko. He first appeared in the comic book "Amazing Fantasy #15" in 1962. Spider-Man is the alter ego of Peter Parker, a student who gained superhuman powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider.

After the death of his Uncle Ben, Peter learned that with great power comes great responsibility. He has since dedicated his life to protecting the people of New York City from various threats.

Peter Parker is known for his intelligence, wit, and determination. Despite facing numerous personal and professional challenges, he continues to be one of the most beloved superheroes in the Marvel Universe.
